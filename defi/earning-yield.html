Earning Yield Safely

DeFi (Decentralized Finance) opens up many opportunities for users to earn yield on their crypto assets. Instead of simply holding tokens in a wallet, you can put them to work and earn passive income. However, it's essential to understand the strategies and risks involved to do this safely.

  • Staking: Lock your tokens in a PoS network (like Ethereum, Solana) to earn staking rewards.
  • Liquidity Mining: Add tokens to a liquidity pool (e.g., Uniswap, PancakeSwap) and earn a share of the trading fees and bonus tokens.
  • Vaults & Aggregators: Use services like Yearn Finance or Beefy Finance to automate yield strategies.
