OG NFT

OG NFTs are commemorative tokens awarded to early adopters or members of a project’s community. Holding an OG NFT often grants access to exclusive benefits such as beta features, governance rights, or future airdrops. These tokens symbolize early support and can carry prestige within a community. Although OG NFTs originally appeared in art and community projects, the concept has expanded to DeFi and blockchain protocols. If you hold an OG NFT, it’s a sign you were involved at the project’s inception, potentially unlocking long-term value and influence.
