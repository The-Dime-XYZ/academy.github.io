What Are Cryptocurrencies?

Cryptocurrencies are digital or virtual currencies that use cryptography for security. Unlike traditional currencies issued by governments (like the US dollar or Euro), cryptocurrencies are typically decentralized and operate on a blockchain‚Äîa distributed ledger enforced by a network of computers, called nodes.

  • Global: Cryptocurrencies can be sent or received anywhere in the world, 24/7.
